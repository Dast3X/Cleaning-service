History
Like any other original place, we have our own, special history. The idea of creating a cleaning service came spontaneously. Once upon a time, the owner of our service, as a student, returned home to complete task on Digital Work. Back home, he noticed a mess and decided to order cleaning services, to his surprise, many refused him, asked for high prices, etc. After that, the idea of ​​creating his own cleaning service haunted him. Soon this place was created (12.12.2012).
